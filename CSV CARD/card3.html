Summary

The Unified Payments Interface (UPI) made a record-high performance in March 2023, after a dip in February. According to the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), UPI processed 868.5 Cr transactions worth INR 14.1 Lakh Cr. Transaction count increased 15.33% MoM, while transaction value jumped 14.1%. UPI logged 753 Cr transactions worth INR 12.36 Lakh Cr in February. The trend shows a strong recovery in UPI transactions in March.
